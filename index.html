What is baseball?

Baseball is a game played by two teams. Both teams get a chance at offense and defense during each inning.
There are 9 inning in a game and there can be extra. Offense is when the team gets a chance at bat and run the bases.
On defense they have 9 feilders where they must try and stop the offense by getting 3 outs.

How to play

To play baseball you need a minimum of 7 players.
Each player must have a chance at bat before a player that has already hit can hit again.
